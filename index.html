    Projects

    Rescue Ready is a flashcard and quiz app tailored for students studying for NREMT exam to become certified EMTs but can also be used a general healthcare study tool. Study topics are split into five categories and color coded to facilitate learning and retention.

    Users have full CRUD functionality on flashcards, flashcard sets and quizzes. Progress and mastery of certain topics can be guaged by the quiz stats provided on quiz submission.
