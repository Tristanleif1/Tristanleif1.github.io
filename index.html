Projects

Rescue Ready

Live Link GitHub

Rescue Ready is a flashcard and quiz app tailored for students studying for the NREMT exam to become nationally certified EMTs but can also be used as a general healthcare study tool. Study topics are split into five categories and color coded to facilitate learning and retention of topics. Users have full CRUD functionality on flashcards, flashcard sets and quizzes. Progress and mastery of certain topics can be guaged by the quiz stats provided on quiz submission. Integrated a dynamic search feature that immeaditely filters the quizzes, flashcards and flashcard sets utilizing React's useEffect hook and array methods providing a lightning fast filtering process to find exactly what you want!

Pledge Palooza

Live Link GitHub

Pledge Palooza is a dynamic crowdfunding platform inspired by Kickstarter, designed to turn innovative ideas into reality through community support. This robust web application allows users to exercise complete CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) functionality for projects, making it easy to launch, manage, and track their creative endeavors. Not only can users start their own projects, but they also have the power to back others' initiatives, offering them the dual role of creators and supporters. A unique feature of Pledge Palooza is its reward system; creators can assign special rewards to various donation tiers, providing backers with tangible tokens of appreciation for their contributions. This system enhances user engagement and fosters a deeper connection between project creators and their supporters. Additionally, users can express their interest and support for projects through a 'like' feature, adding a social dimension to the platform.
